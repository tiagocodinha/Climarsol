About us

The Story of Climarsol

A mechanical carpentry company with family roots, combining technology, detail and close client support.

Who we are

Climarsol is a family-owned company dedicated to bespoke carpentry, founded in 1977. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to create unique pieces that reflect each client’s needs and personality.

Every project is handled with maximum care and attention to detail, from the first site visit to final installation. We work with high-quality woods, reliable hardware and premium finishes to ensure durability, comfort and architectural coherence.

Our team supports the client at every stage — from 3D design and material selection to technical solutions and after-sales service — ensuring a simple, transparent and stress-free process.

How we work

1. Site visit & measurements

We begin with an on-site visit and detailed measurements, listening carefully to the client’s needs, daily habits and usage goals before proposing solutions.

2. Bespoke design

We develop the technical and visual project based on real measurements, chosen materials and integration with the surrounding space — refining until aesthetics, ergonomics and budget align perfectly.

3. Production & installation

Production takes place in our mechanical carpentry workshop, ensuring precision and consistency, complemented by skilled manual work during on-site assembly, adjustment and finishing.
